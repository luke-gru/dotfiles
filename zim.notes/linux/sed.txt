====== sed ======
The Stream Editor

**Substitute:**
$sed s/day/night/ <old >new
$sed **'**s/day/night/**'** <old >new								<<- like ack
$sed 's**|**/usr/local/bin**|**/common/bin**|**' <old >new			<<- different delimiter (like vi)
