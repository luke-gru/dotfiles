**Unit Testing: **//Ruby 1.9 comes with MiniTest instead of Test::Unit//
1) require "minitest/unit", and use the MiniTest functionality.										<<- autorun off
2) require "test/unit", and use MiniTest with the Test::Unit compatibility layer.					<<- autorun on
