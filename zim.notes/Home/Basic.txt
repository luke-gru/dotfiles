====== Basic ======

* Controller instance variables are accessible to the views.
**http://localhost/store/index** = StoreController.new.index
* views and controller actions share the same name. 
//The views are in app/views///
//The controllers are in app/controllers///

* want one layout for the whole of the application? Put this in ApplicationController:

layout "store"
