====== State ======

**State is used to move state-dependent operations to objects through delegation and**
**aggregation.**


Context<>------------- State[abstract]  => Concretestate1, Concretestate2
request()                          handle()


state->handle()

Ex: TCPConnection (context)
       TCPEstablished (concretestate), TCPListening (concretestate), TCPClosed (concretestate)

Context may pass itself as argument
