====== Rspec factory ======

Creates objects needed in TEST mode (like users) to move the tests along.

**Example from sample_app in sessions_controller_spec.rb : **

describe SessionsController do

  .
  .
  .
	describe "DELETE 'destroy'" do

    
		it "should sign a user out" do

			test_sign_in(Factory(:user))
			delete :destroy
			controller.should_not be_signed_in
			response.should redirect_to(root_path)

		end

	end

end




*Factorygirl is a good rubygem for this
