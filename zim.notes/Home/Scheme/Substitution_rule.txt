====== Substitution rule ======

**To evaluate a combination expression:**
1. evaluate the operator to get the procedure
2. evaluate the operands to get the arguments
3. Apply the procedure to the arguments
4. Copy the body of the procedure, substituting the arguments supplied with the formal parameters (symbols) of the procedure
5. Evaluate the resulting new body. 

**To evaluate a conditional expression (IF, COND):**
1. evaluate the predicate expression
2. if the predicate expression is true
		evaluate the consequent

    otherwise

		evaluate the alternative
