How much does it cost?

It's free!

Can I bring my guy/girl friend(s)?

Yeah, as long as there's an interested girl in your party then you're more than welcome. Just keep in mind that this is primarily for girls, so you might want to check with me first before your group of guy friends shows up.

I'm in highschool. Can I come?

All ages are welcome.

I want to learn web design. Is that what this is?

Not really. Although there is some design involved, Ruby on Rails allows us, as web developers, to create dynamic content on the web. That means keeping things in databases and giving cookies to web browsers and all that jazz. But, of course, we will be making sites and therefore will be doing at least some design in the process.
