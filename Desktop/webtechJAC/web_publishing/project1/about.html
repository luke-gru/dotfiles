The Teacher(s)

There will hopefully be more than one teacher, but for now there's me. Hopefully, as the workshop progresses there will be others as well. The Ruby and Rails communities are great, and the people involved will definitely be able to help you out. Since you're probably just getting your feet wet, this Ruby quickstart tutorial is as good a place to start as any.

Level

Beginners are encouraged to come. In fact, it's for beginners. We won't assume any prior knowledge of web development or web design. That doesn't mean that you can't already have, say, some HTML or CSS knowledge. We won't be turning down anyone (unless they're a lone guy). It is, however, recommended to have a good grasp of the English language and some strong logical skills. If you're a good debater or good at doing Sudokus, you can bet you'll be good at picking up Ruby on Rails.
