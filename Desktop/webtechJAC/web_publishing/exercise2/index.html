My Hobbies

Hey, my name's Luke. And yes, I'm glad you asked; I do have hobbies. Unfortunately, it doesn't seem like I've been actually doing any of them that recently. But then I googled 'quotes on hobbies' and was relieved to find that at least one brainyquote-worthy-person seemed to actually think this a good thing.

A hobby is only fun if you do not have time to do it.
   --Leo Beenhakker

Now, I'm not sure I agree with this, but at least Mr. Beenhakker tried.

But on with it, you say! I agree. My hobbies include:

  • Playing guitar
    • Jazz, blues, rock... I like it all
  • Seeing movies
    • One of my favourite movies is North by NorthWest (I know, very original)
  • Going to concerts
    • Montreal's a great city for concert-goers
  • Snowboarding
    • Because I had to throw in the one sport to mix things up
  • Programming with Ruby
    • The first program I made was a reminder to walk my dog called Ruminder

The three year-round staples of my hobby-life (above) in a nutshell: Sitting, sitting and... more sitting. What will I do when the snow melts and I can no longer snowboard? If the past is a good predictor of the future, the smart money is on more sitting activities involving sitting.
