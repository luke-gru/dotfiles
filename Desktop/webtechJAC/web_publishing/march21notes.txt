Notes

font-style: italic || oblique
text-decoration: underline || overline || line-through || inherit
line-height: 50px, 1.5 (1.5 x size of font), 2.0 = double-spacing
text-align: left, right, center, justify

background-image: url(images/bg.gif); (apply to any single element)
background-repeat: no-repeat || repeat-x || repeat-y || repeat (both)
background-attachment: fixed || scroll || inherit (move with content or not)
background-position: center || top || bottom || right || left (and variations thereof (bottom-right, top-left, etc...), good practise to always select a background-color also, in case the image doesn't show up

Box Model:
padding (padding-top, -left, -right, -bottom), space b/t element's content and its border
padding-right means left-most border of box will have more space between it and the content.
padding: 10px; (4 sides)
padding: 5 px 10px; (x axis, y axis)
padding: 5px 10px 5 px; (top, left-right, bottom)
padding: 4 numbers (top, left, bottom, right) => counter-clockwise


content => padding => border => margin

border:
border-width: 
border-style: solid || dotted || dashed || double || inset || outset || groove
border-color: 
(mix 'n' match) => border-top-color || border-bottom-width etc...
border-width: 10px 5px; (x, y)

margin: works same way as border, but outside the content of the box

overflow: content outside of the border of the element
default overflow is visible
overflow: hidden;
overflow: scroll; (scrollbars there no matter what, even if no overflow)
overflow: auto; (scrollbars if needed)

div {float: left; } will float all divs in order of their source appearance
