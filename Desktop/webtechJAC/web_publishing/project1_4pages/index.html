Only Girls?

There aren't enough girls (and women) who like to program. But Ruby was designed so that the programming language itself is similar to a natural language, like French or English. This makes it more suitable for quick learning, and for non-technical people to grasp the concepts behind it.

Are you assuming girls are non-technical? How dare you!

No! You're twisting my words! All are welcome at the Girls_on_Rails => 'mtl' meetups, but you're only allowed to come if you're accompanied by an interested girl/woman. Even though women have made huge dents in other traditionally male-dominated fields and activities, it seems that web development and computer programming have been left behind. So spread the word, tell all your girl friends and come ride the Rails!
